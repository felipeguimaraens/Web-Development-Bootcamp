Day of birth invitation note

we desire to invite thou to mine day of birth party on going to be at ides march of 1856.

what thou should'st bring

  1. Hats
  2. Balloons
  3. Cake
  4. Tea

Thou might not but hit together to course these rules:

  • never smile to camera
  • forbear at all cost wounds! (because penicillin is going to be discovered only 70 years from now)
